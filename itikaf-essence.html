The Essence of I`tikaaf

üôé M.Q Atanda     ‚è±Ô∏è April 27, 2021

From `Aaishah (RA), it was reported that:

"The prophet, Sollal-laahu `alayhi, usually seclude (inside the mosque) during the last Ten Days in the month of Romadoon till Allaah, the most high, took his life. Then after, his wives continued (the i'tikaaf) after his demise.

And in another report,

The prophet usually secludes (in the mosque) in the month of Romadoon. So, whenever he finishes his prayer at morning, he returns back to his seclude position."

(Both narration was reported by Bukhooriy)

general meaning

In this hadiith, it is established that:

Al-I'tikaaf (means Seclusion) was commanded by Allaah and His prophet. On this also, the prophet was reported to have commanded the search for Laylatul-qodri throughout the last TEN Days of Romadoon. The prophet (saw) engaged in this act of 'seclusion' till he had breath.

However, his wives continued after his death, and they stood on the legacies he left behind. The legacies include, among others:

  • Allaahumma innaka 'afuw-wun tuhib-bul-'afwa fa-'fu'an-nii(naa)
  • engagement in lots of supplication and Tahaajud

extracted facts from the hadiith

  • Establishment of I'tikaaf. It is regarded as the Sunnah the prophet (saw).
  • That the prophet enjoined it to be observed at the last ten days in the month of Blessings. And for the purpose of Laylatul-qodri.
  • That I'tikaaf continues every year during Romadon.
  • I'tikaaf requires luzuum (permanent seclusion) inside a mosque throughout the ten days.
  • That, the appropriate time to enter I'tikaaf is after Sub-hi ( and latest before maghrib, 20th of Romadoon).
  • That, we should distant ourselves from worldly things.
  • And to crown it all, that we stand for tahaajud stand for tahaajud, make our requests to Allaah and repeating the prayer ALLOOHUMMA INNAKA `AFUW-WUN TUHIB-BUL-`AFWA FA`FU `AN-NII {several times}
